Mental Wellness

Mental Health is the other aspect of our total well-being. Mental Health refers to one's emotional wellbeing, and how they cope with their circumstances or surroundings. Mental Health is comprised of three components: cognitive health, emotional health, and behavioral health. 

Learn More

Physical Wellness

Physical Health only represents one dimension of total well-being. Physical Health refers to the state of your physical body, and how well it is functioning. There are four major components to improve physical well-being: an active lifestyle, healthy diet, disease and hygiene protection, and physical fitness.

What Sets Us Apart?

We are a virtual community aiming to provide assistance to all those seeking assistance with mental and/or physical wellness. We know and value that health is not limited to any one thing; rather, it is a combination of multiple aspects, and not everyone is fortunate to be well in all aspects. Our application allows you to select what you need help with and it guides yous step by step. If you couldn't find what you need or would like to talk to us, you can contact us with the chat function.
